FIRE DOOR GALLERY 

FD30
Fire Door

Fire Door that provides a 30mins protection from smoke, and toxic gases. Its resistance rating can endure exposure to flames, heat, and smoke for 30 minutes before its integrity is compromised

FD60
Fire Door

These are a specific type of fire door that provides 60 minutes of fire resistance. These doors are designed to withstand exposure to flames, heat, and smoke for up to 60 minutes, giving occupants more time to evacuate a building in the event of a fire.

FD90
Fire Door

Has 90 minutes of fire resistance. Its extended fire resistance, enhanced safety features, strategic placement, regulatory compliance, property protection, insurance considerations, and peace of mind make it an essential investment for building owners who prioritize occupant safety and property preservation.

FD120
Fire Door

These doors are designed to withstand the effects of fire, heat, and smoke for an impressive 120 minutes. This extended fire resistance duration provides invaluable time for occupants to evacuate safely and for firefighters to respond effectively.

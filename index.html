About Ojn

OJN Ltd undertakes the necessary inspections on all your existing fire doors to ascertain if they meet compliance and provides advice on what works and repairs to do for failed fire doors to meet with the Regulatory Reform (fire Safety) Order 2005.

We offer our services to a variety of sectors such as:
Education, Healthcare, HMO’s, Government and Local Authority buildings, Public and Social Housing, Leisure and Hospitality facilities, Retail stores and complex, Industrial sector/complex.
